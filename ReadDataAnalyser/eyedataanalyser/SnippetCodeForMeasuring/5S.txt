And as she was thus forsaken by all the world, she went forth into the open
country, trusting in the good God.

Then a poor man met her, who said, “Ah, give me something to eat, I am so
hungry!”

She reached him the whole of her piece of bread, and said, “May God bless
it to your use,” and went onward.

Then came a child who moaned and said, “My head is so cold, give me
something to cover it with.”

So she took off her hood and gave it to him.
